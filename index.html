Certificates

Our certificate are being issued after completeting a level. engineeringstudents get their certificate after completing a 3 months course and passed it, while business students must complete a semester which is 6 months.

Diploma

Enginnering students must pass all their trimesters in order to obtain a diploma which is N1-N6 which is 2years. while businees students will have to do N4-N6 which is 1 year 6 months in order to obtain their diploma.

Matric Rewrite/ Upgrade

We offer matric Rewrite, but students need to apply to any high school of their choice for exam center, then springfield will offer them with a Quality teaching and making sure they obtain the results their are wishing for. so students will choose a high school that will register them to do rewrite then the college cover the teaching part.
